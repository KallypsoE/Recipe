Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients Directions
2 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil In a large skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Add chicken and season with salt and pepper. Cook until golden and cooked through, 8 minutes per side. Let rest 10 minutes, then slice.
2 boneless skinless chicken breasts Add milk, broth, and garlic to skillet. Season with salt and pepper and bring to a simmer. Add fettuccine, stirring frequently for about 3 minutes. Let cook until al dente, 8 minutes more.
Kosher salt Stir in heavy cream and Parmesan until combined. Simmer until sauce thickens.
Freshly ground black pepper Remove from heat and stir in sliced chicken. Garnish with parsley.

Ground Beef Tacos

Ingredients Directions
2 teaspoons vegetable oil Heat the oil in a large pan over medium high heat.
1 pound lean ground beef Add the ground beef and break up with a spatula.
1/2 cup onion (finely chopped) Add the onion to the pan.
1 tablespoon chili powder Cook, stirring occasionally, until beef is done and onion is soft, 5-6 minutes. Drain off any excess fat.
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder Add the chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, red pepper flakes, oregano, smoked paprika, cumin, salt and pepper to the pan. Stir to coat the meat in the seasonings.
1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (optional) Add the tomatoes to the pan and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano Spoon the beef into the taco shells and add toppings such as lettuce, tomatoes and cheese. Serve immediately.

Lasagna

Ingredients Directions
1 1/2 (16-ounce) boxes lasagna noodles Bring a large pot of well-salted water to a boil. Working batches, cook the lasagna noodles until they are soft and pliable but not limp, 6 to 7 minutes. Remove the pasta from the boiling water and lay flat on a sheet tray to cool. Reserve.
Olive oil Coat a large saute pan with olive oil and bring to medium-high heat. Add the sausage and cook until brown and crumbly. Remove from pan and reserve on paper towels.
1 pound bulk Italian sausage Ditch the fat from the pan and add new olive oil along with 2 cloves of garlic and a pinch of crushed red pepper. Bring the pan to medium-high heat. When the garlic becomes golden and very aromatic, remove it from the heat and discard. Toss in the mushrooms and season them with salt. Cook the mushrooms until they are soft and wilted and dark brown, 4 to 5 minutes. Remove from pan and reserve. Repeat this process with the remaining garlic and the zucchini.
4 cloves garlic, smashed, divided In a small bowl, combine the ricotta, 1/2 the Parmigiano-Reggiano, the eggs, and the basil. Mix to combine well and season with salt.
Crushed red pepper flakes Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
1 (12-ounce) package cremini mushrooms, stems removed, caps sliced In the bottom of a 9 by 13-inch deep-dish baking dish, add a couple ladlefuls of sauce and spread out in an even layer. Arrange a layer of the lasagna noodles to completely cover the sauce. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the pasta. Place a layer of pasta going in the other direction as the first layer (this will give a little more stability). Spread a light layer of sauce on the pasta and sprinkle 1/3 of the sausage over the sauce, repeat this process with 1/3 of the mushrooms and zucchini.
1 zucchini, cut in 1/2 lengthwise and cut on the bias Sprinkle a layer of mozzarella and some of the remaining Parmigiano-Reggiano over the veggies. Repeat these layers until all the ingredients have been used up or the pan is full. Be sure that there is a layer of pasta on top covered with sauce and sprinkled with mozzarella and Parmigiano. Cover with foil.
2 cups ricotta Place the lasagna on a baking sheet and bake in the oven until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, about 1 hour 15 minutes, removing the foil for the last 15 minutes of cooking. Let cool for 20 minutes before slicing.
2 cups grated Parmigiano-Reggiano, divided Note: For optimal slicing, make and bake the lasagna the day before. Heat it up again before slicing.

Marinara Sauce Ingredients Marinara Sauce Directions
1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil Coat a large saucepot with olive oil and add the pancetta. Bring the pot to medium-high heat and cook the pancetta for 4 to 5 minutes. Add the onions, season generously with salt, and stir to coat with the olive oil. Cook the onions for 6 to 7 minutes, stirring frequently; the onions should become very soft and aromatic but have no color. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 to 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
1/4 pound pancetta, diced Pass the tomatoes through a food mill. Be sure to pass all of the pulp through the holes leaving only the stems and the seeds, and be sure to scrape the pulp off of the bottom of the food mill. That's all of the big money stuff! Add the tomatoes to the pot and rinse out one of the empty tomato cans with water and add that water to the pot (about 2 to 3 cups).
2 large Spanish onions, cut into 1/4-inch dice Season generously with salt and taste it; tomatoes take a lot of salt. Season in baby steps and taste every step of the way. Cook the sauce for 2 to 3 hours, stirring occasionally and tasting frequently.
Kosher salt Use the sauce right away on pasta or for any other tomato sauce need. This sauce can also be cooled and stored in the fridge for a few days or it freezes really well.

Sage Sausage and Cream Cheese Dip

Ingredients Directions
2 lbs. sage sausage Brown sage sausage in a pan. Drain off the excess grease.
8 oz. cream cheese In a crockpot, on high heat, add the sausage and cream cheese. Drain Rotel diced tomatoes with habaneros, add it to the pot. Stir gently until everything is combined. Lower the heat to low.
10 oz. Rotel diced tomatoes with habaneros Serve warm with some veggies and chips.

Zucchnini Bread

Ingredients Directions
1 1/2 cups grated zucchini-lightly packed (do not drain liquid) Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a 9x5 inch loaf pan with cooking spray.
1 cup granulated sugar In a large bowl, add the grated zucchini, sugar, brown sugar, applesauce, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Whisk until well combined.
1/4 cup packed light brown sugar Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon. Stir just until no dry flour remains, trying not to over mix.
1/2 cup unsweetened applesauce Pour the batter into the loaf pan. Bake for 50 to 54 minutes. A toothpick inserted into the center of the bread should come out with moist crumbs on it.
1/3 cup vegetable oil (or your preferred cooking oil) Cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Remove from the pan and transfer to a wire cooling rack to cool completely before slicing.
2 large eggs Store covered in the refrigerator. *This bread is best served after it has been refrigerated for at least 12 hours.
